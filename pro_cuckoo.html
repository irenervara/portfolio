Tear Down Product Analysis. A studio without touching the object.

What if you are ask to disassemble an object? I was told that would be the only way to understand the behavior on the product, how it has been built and which are the materials its composed by.
Ok! I could not believe that was the only way, so, I decided to detect how far I may go not only without disassembling it, but without touching it!
