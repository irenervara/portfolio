About Me | Portfolio

I am an architect from Spain. I received my Master in Architecture in 2015 from San Pablo CEU University, being awarded with the ‘Best Final Thesis Project of 2015’. After finishing my mayor, I decided to get out of my comfort zone and I moved to South Korea where I was living and working with passion during some years, gaining knowledge and team work experience with multicultural groups as well as exploring new resources related to the technology and society.

With the aim of expanding my training, I returned to Spain to do the Master in City and Technology taught by IAAC (Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia), continuing with my interest in new ways to approach architecture, finding out the unknown layers to add in our cities and designing a new life system for the society.

Now, new life and new projects are coming... Let´s have fun!
