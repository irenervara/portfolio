About Me | Portfolio

Irene is an architect from Spain. She received her Master in Architecture in 2015 from San Pablo CEU University, being awarded with the ‘Best Final Thesis Project of 2015’. Since then, she has been living and working in South Korea, gaining knowledge and team work experience with multicultural groups as well as exploring new resources related to the technology and society, fields which she feels more and more identified as the time goes by.
Now, she just came back to her origin country to be part of the Master in City & Technology program imparted by IAAC, continuing with her interest in new ways to approach architecture, finding out the unknown layers to add in our cities and designing a new life system for the society.
